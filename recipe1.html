Keto Avocado Egg Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise (sugar-free)
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives or parsley (optional)

Nutritional Information (Approximate per serving)

  • Calories: 200
  • Protein: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fats: 8g

Steps

  1. Place the eggs in a small pot and cover them with water.
  2. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let the eggs simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the eggs from the hot water and place them in a bowl of ice water to cool. Once cooled, peel the eggs and chop them into small pieces.
  5. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  6. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth.
  7. Add the chopped eggs to the mashed avocado.
  8. Mix in the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. If desired, add chopped fresh chives or parsley for extra flavor and garnish.
  11. Serve the avocado egg salad on a bed of lettuce, in a low-carb wrap, or simply enjoy it on its own.
