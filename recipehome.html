Keto Avocado Egg Salad

Egg Ravioli is a unique and inventive take on the traditional pasta dish, tailored specifically for those following a ketogenic diet. This dish replaces conventional pasta dough with delicate sheets made from eggs and Parmesan cheese, creating a low-carb yet indulgent base for the ravioli.
